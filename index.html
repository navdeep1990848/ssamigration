Our history

  • June 2009

    Our Beginning in India

    SSA Migration started in Patiala, Punjab with one small office to provide quality English coaching and Visa services. "None of us ever thought of how big this would become or where this company would go five years from now. But halfway through, we began to think that we’re onto something as we were getting students from all over the city and nearby villages.

  • August 2015

    Australia Branch Opened

    Due to the rapid business growth we have experienced in India over last 5 years, we planned to open an office in Australia. In 2015 we registered with MARA, MIA, ISANA and MMA. Hence we started providing Education counselling and Migration assistance to our clients.

  • November 2017

    New Zealand Branch Opened

    The number of new branches isn’t the most surprising aspect of this story, but rather the timing and the markets that are particularly interesting. In our 8 years journey we focused a lot on providing quality services rather than expanding

  • May 2019

    Samana & Yamunanagar Branch Opened

    The opening of these two branches was always in pipeline for quite some time in view of rising demand SSA Migration clients in this region. Our plan is to expand in north India first before setting foot in South India.
